# Overview
Brighton has a very distinguished approach when it comes to its recruitment process. First of all, they mostly recruit young players in leagues like EFL Championship, Eredivisie, Belgian Pro League etc. A pattern that can often be found is that most of the young players are loaned out __% of the time, most likely because the Brighton board and managers want them to gain experience across variety of leagues which develops adaptability of environments for the players. Now this is very important for Brighton because it is one of the only team in the Premier league where every year, they gain more in transfers than they spend, meaning that current players are expected to play with new teammates very frequently.
